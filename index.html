Teacher note: The numeric details, formulas, and classroom scenarios are warmup-friendly approximations generated from the headline set, not claims of exact news data. Public feed loading uses multiple attempts and a recent local browser cache, but manual headline paste remains the most reliable option when networks or feed providers block requests. A relevance loop tries alternate skills and alternate headlines before any card appears. If a formula or task does not have a defensible connection to the selected headline, the card tells the teacher to choose another headline or click Generate warmups again instead of forcing a weak connection. A moral filter blocks calculations about weapons, casualties, injury rates, attack mechanics, biological death/survival models, or other harm-adjacent quantities when headlines involve violence, death, injury, legal trauma, or other sensitive events. Click Generate warmups again to get a different version when a prompt is not a good fit. Edit any number, claim, or framing before printing when precision matters.
